Coffee Bun Recipe

Ingredients

Dough Ingredients

These dough ingredients can be used to produce about 12 pcs

  • 250g high protein flour
  • 5g yeast
  • 1 tbsp milk powder
  • 50g sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 90ml cold water
  • 25g margarine

Topping Igredients

  • 55g granulated sugar
  • 50g margarine
  • 60g medium protein flour
  • 1 tsp instant coffee
  • 1 egg
  • Mocca pasta
